Styles to Try

Here are some hairstyles for inspiration.

The Afro

This timeless hairstyle can be achieved with just a leave in conditioner and water. See Night Time Routine for details on how to achieve this effortless look.

Crown Updo

The crown updo is obtained by sectioning off the front part of the hair, where bangs would be. This section is flat-twisted to the side of the head. The rest of the hair is gathered into a ponytail and the tail of the flat twist is bobby pinned to the side of the updo.

Mini Twists

Mini twists are one of the most low-maintenance hairstyle that does not require much upkeep. Using a leave-in conditioner, sealing cream, and essential oils, separate your hair into small sections and twist to the end. Let the hair air-dry and sleep with a satin bonnet or scarf. The style can be kept for two to four weeks depending on the size of the twists. Smaller twists will last longer.
